About me

My name is Mohammad, I am a front-end developer. For the past 7 months, I've been studying with Kodehode, a front-end focused course. I don't want to stop there and want to be a full stack developer, which is what I'm currently working on.

All my life I have been interested in modern technologies and this led me to study IT. In a short time, I was able to master everything that is needed for front-end development and realized that I had found my calling.

I used to be an entrepreneur, I had several successful businesses and projects in the service and entertainment sectors. Thanks to this, I was able to gain experience in commerce and marketing. Now I have a great desire to combine all my skills and create something big.

In my work I am competitive, honest and confident. In life I'm a very ambitious person and this has helped me to reach new hights in my goals. One of my future goals is that I would like to achieve in my life is to get a pilots license.

What do I like to do? I love traveling, spending time with family and friends. I also love video games, art and of course coding.

What do I consider important in life? I find it important to be honest, spiritually strong, and be helpful to my family and those around me.
